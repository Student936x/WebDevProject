Best Mario Kart 8 Deluxe Tracks

(In my Humble Opinion)

  1. Baby Park
  • The greatest of all the Mario Kart tracks and a Double Dash classic!
  • 7 laps in around one big loop. It's pure chaos!
  • First appeared in Mario Kart Double Dash.
  1. Wii Rainbow Road
  • Without a doubt, this track is the best Rainbow Road we have ever seen in a Mario Kart game.
  • Full of twists and turns with very few straightaways.
  • As noted in the title of this track, this course first appeared in Mario Kart Wii.
  1. Mount Wario
  • It stands out from other tracks because of its continuous nature. It doesn't loop at all.
  • The race starts from the inside of a blimp instead of at a traditional starting line.
  • An original track from Mario Kart 8.
  1. Coconut Mall
  • This course easily has one of the best and catchiest soundtracks in the Mario Kart series.
  • It is loosely themed around Super Mario Sunshine.
  • First appeared in Mario Kart Wii.
  1. Moo Moo Meadows
  • The biggest course obstacles are cows and you are racing in the country. What's there not to like?
  • While not the most complicated track out there, its simplicity is part of what makes it great.
  • First appeared in Mario Kart Wii.
